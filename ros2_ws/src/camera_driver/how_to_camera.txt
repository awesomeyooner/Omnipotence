To see IO
v4l2-ctl -d /dev/video0 --list-formats-ext

List services:
ros2 service list

To send values to service (make sure to source this ws tho)
ros2 service call /camera/configure_camera camera_driver/srv/ConfigureCamera "{width: 320, height: 240, fps: 30}"
ros2 service call /camera/configure_camera camera_driver/srv/ConfigureCamera "{width: 320, height: 240, fps: 30}"
ros2 service call /camera/reconfigure_defaults camera_driver/srv/ReconfigureDefaults "{}"
