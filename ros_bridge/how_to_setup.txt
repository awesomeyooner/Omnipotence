https://github.com/ros2/ros1_bridge/blob/master/doc/index.rst

building ros2:
. /opt/ros/foxy/setup.bash
cd into your ros2_msgs_ws
colcon build 

building ros1:
. /opt/ros/noetic/setup.bash
cd into your ros1_msgs_ws
catkin_make_isolated --install

building the bridge:
first clone, so cd into your bridge_ws and cd into src (make sure to clone foxy)
git clone -b foxy https://github.com/ros2/ros1_bridge.git

. /opt/ros/noetic/setup.bash
. /opt/ros/foxy/setup.bash
. ~/ros1_msgs_ws/install_isolated/setup.bash
. ~/ros2_msgs_ws/install/local_setup.bash

cd into bridge_ws
colcon build --packages-select ros1_bridge --cmake-force-configure
